Location

Why
Sighnaghi?

From an investor perspective, Signaghi offers attractive residential fundamentals:

  • Limited supply of organized apartment housing
  • Demand driven primarily by permanent residents
  • Low risk of large-scale speculative oversupply
  • Competitive construction economics
  • Favorable absorption for well-priced residential units

These characteristics support a conservative, execution-focused development strategy.

Execution

Development
Phasing

Aurra Residences will be developed through a block-by-block construction approach, comprising three residential blocks of approximately 5,000 sqm each, delivering 300+ Apartments across the full project.

Each block represents a separate development phase and is executed sequentially.

  • Estimated Apartments: 90–110 units
  • Purpose: Price discovery, demand validation, and project launch
  • Focus: Pre-launch buyers and early public sales

Positioning

Strategic Assessment

Strengths

  • Freehold ownership of a 10,000 sqm land parcel
  • First organized residential apartment development in the region in decades
  • Phased, block-by-block execution reduces capital and market risk
  • Modern construction standards compared to existing housing stock
  • Strong alignment with real end-user housing demand

Weaknesses

  • Regional location with limited historical large-scale development activity
  • Market still developing modern apartment culture
  • Absorption dependent on gradual buyer education
  • Dependence on phased sales momentum in early stages

Opportunities

  • Significant unmet demand for modern apartments in the Sighnaghi region
  • First-mover advantage allows early pricing leadership
  • Rising lifestyle, tourism, and second-home interest in Kakheti
  • Ability to replicate the development model in other Tier-2 and Tier-3 towns
  • Pre-launch buyer program enables early price appreciation

Threats

  • Market slowdown risk mitigated by phased inventory release
  • Pricing volatility managed through early sales and price discovery
  • Construction risk reduced through experienced local contractors
  • Capital exposure reduced through sales-led funding in later phases
